ASTEROIDS

Exploring the Skies, Protecting the Earth

Introduction

Asteroids, the silent sentinels of our solar system, hold the potential for catastrophic impact on Earth. Though the chances of a major collision are rare, the consequences could be apocalyptic, echoing the ancient impacts that once reshaped the surface of our planet and led to mass extinctions. Modern science allows us to track these celestial wanderers, yet many remain undetected, lurking in the shadows of space. Understanding and monitoring asteroids is not just a matter of scientific curiosity but a crucial safeguard against potential future disasters. As we stand on the brink of potential celestial calamities, the study of asteroids is not only about exploring the cosmos but also about protecting our own world from the unknown dangers of the universe.
